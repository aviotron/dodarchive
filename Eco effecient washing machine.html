Workshop on eco efficient washing machine

One week workshop on "Designing for eco-efficiency in washing machines- Advanced Design" for Master of Design students.

Workshop instructor:
Shujoy Chakraborty: Design Engineer at Whirlpool Corporation/ PhD Candidate IDEA- Industrial Design & Engineering for innovation
Avinash Shende: Assistant Professor, Department of Design, IIT Guwahati

Objective:
To achieve a design language for communicating eco-efficient technologies in the washing machine appliance platform. Lectures: History of development of Washing Machines, Eco Efficiency, Sustainability Process: Detailed discussion with individual teams regarding Concept Development, Finalization, CAD model, Rendering,

Presentation Outcome:
Advanced concepts which exploited various typological and use-mode innovations for near future washing machines.
